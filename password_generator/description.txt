Steps:
1. Add shebang: #!/usr/bin/env python
2. Add to execute file: chmod +x generatepassword.py
3. Remove the extension of the file: mv generatepassword.py generatepassword
4. Move it to /usr/local/bin: mv ./generatepassword /usr/local/bin

Usage:
generatepassword -l 15
generatepassword --length 15
